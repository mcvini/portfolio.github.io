The man behind the works

As a video game design student and enthusiast I've always had a passion for creating interesting characters. My biggest passion is the process of designing a character, be for a game or for animation, and watching it come to life through the development of its origins and creating a journey for it to face. Through studying video game design I've had to opportunity to work alongside many interesting people and have had the chance to work on several different kinds of projects ranging from developing illustrations for a physical card game to the designing and coding of online games.

I specialize mainly in the development of characters however I also have an expertice in designing world items and illustrations such as logos and icons. Recently I've been broadening my skills through designing my own game levels and scenary. Furthermore I've also an expertise in using several visual design tools such as photoshop and illustrator, I've had some experience using open modelling softwares such as blender and game engines of the likes of unreal engine 4.

While I'm not diligently working on a project, personal or external, you can often find me online gaming with friends, sketching out new charater ideas on my sketchbook, playing football, or working out at the gym. My main mission as a game design student is to get the opportunity to work on and develop a character that will be remembered as an iconic figure of game design.
